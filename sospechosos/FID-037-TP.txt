Human-AI interaction has emerged as a critical focal point in the advancement of more responsive and empathetic technology. Within this context, the utilization of artificial empathy strategies holds particular significance, given its potential to enhance customer experiences affectively and socially. This research endeavors to delve into the optimization of human-AI interactions through the application of artificial empathy strategies, aiming to enhance affective and social customer experiences. Employing a qualitative research approach, this study conducts a thorough review of various studies and related literature. The data sources encompass journals, articles, and books pertinent to the research topic. The research findings indicate that the incorporation of artificial empathy strategies in human-AI interactions holds significant promise in elevating the quality of interactions and customer experiences. Leveraging technologies such as natural language processing, emotion recognition, and sentiment analysis enables AI to respond more accurately and sensitively to user needs and emotions.
