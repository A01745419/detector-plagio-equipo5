Artificial intelligence (AI) is rapidly developing and spreading its application, becoming an integral part of our daily lives. In fact, AI has transformed the learning process for people. However, its integration into the educational sector has encountered challenges and ethical issues. The purpose of this study is to analyze the opportunities, benefits, and challenges of AI in education. A systematic review method was used to review available and relevant literature, aiming to identify the current research focus and provide educators and future research directions with a comprehensive understanding of AI technology in education. Findings revealed that the adoption of AI in education has progressed significantly in developed countries, with most research gaining popularity during the Industry 4.0 era. The study also discusses other challenges and provides recommendations.
